Wedding Party

Amanda Strawn

Maid of Honor

Amanda and I met in high school Spanish class. We decided to be twins for twin day because we had the same pair of pants. Over the years we've enjoyed going to movies and concerts together, including multiple trips to Vans Warped Tour. Although on different coasts, we will always discuss the latest TV show episodes we're both watching and books we're both reading. I loved being a part of her wedding in October 2014 and can't wait for her to be a part of mine!

Rachel Wharton

Bridesmaid

Rachel is my oldest childhood friend. Her parents introduced my parents and we've been friends since we were born. We would spend New Years Eve and Fourth of July together with our families every year growing up. One of my favorite memories with Rachel was when we decided to color ourselves head to tow with markers. Her mom made sure to get plenty of picture evidence to use for embarrassment when we were older. I had the honor of being in Rachel's wedding in September 2014 and I am so excited for her to be in mine!

Anthony Anastasi

Best Man

Anthony and I have been friends since I was 5 years old. We lived in the same neighborhood growing up and spent almost every day together. We would spend countless hours playing sports, having lemonade stands, building forts in our backyards, and playing video games. Although Anthony recently moved to Atlanta, we are still able to stay very close and talk frequently. Anthony has always been like a brother to me, and I am so excited that he is able to be the Best Man in my wedding.

Kevin Yoakum

Groomsman

Kevin and I became friends after being in the same high school DECA class. We would spend almost every day after school either going bowling, with our own bowling ball and shoes of course, or shredding the guitar on Rock Band. Kevin and I were always trying to think of ideas to get rich quick, and our most successful business venture was selling laser pointers to students in our classes. Unfortunately, we were never too profitable. Kevin and I still spend a lot of time at the bowling alley, and it will be great to have him in the wedding.
