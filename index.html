Travel

Where to stay

Hotel block

We have a hotel room block with a Courtyard Marriott in Kirkland that is about 10 minutes from the wedding venue. You can book rooms for a discounted rate here. There is no limit on how many rooms we can book they just open in increments, so if the link isn't showing availability let us know and we will contact the hotel. The last day to book is 9/23/17 and rates range from $139-$169.

Other nearby hotels

There are also plenty of other nearby hotels that are very reasonable. These are in the Woodinville / Bothell area and are about 10 minutes away.

  • Hampton Inn
  • Country Inn
  • Marriott Residence Inn

Airbnb

There are a ton of unique options on Airbnb and you can find a lot of great deals for entire homes and condos. If you are looking for something close to the venue look in the Woodinville or Kirkland areas. The venue is right by the main area of wineries. Reach out if you want to know about any specific area!

Upscale lodge

If you are looking to stay somewhere more upscale there is an adorable lodge in walking distance from the venue. There is a spa and some renound restaurants. Check out Willows Lodge here.
